ABOUT US

The company was founded in 1972 by Mr. M C John a retired senior bureaucrat in the Kerala government. It is currently led by Mr. Simon John and his team of eminent directors. The company is a pioneer in the ice cream industry in South India. The first factory was started in Kochi and today has over 10 factories and other ancillary units spread across South India.

OUR VISION

To make enjoyable ice cream experiences affordable and available to all.

OUR MISSION

To manufacture international quality ice cream products at best-in-class pricing and make them available everywhere through a well-placed cold chain network.

OUR VALUES

We focus on creating value for the consumers who can see and choose what is really best for them.
